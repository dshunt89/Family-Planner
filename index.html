📖 How to Use

Getting Started
  1. Set your Academic Year Start Date in Data Input
  2. Update Term Dates to match your school
  3. Enter all Bank Holidays for the year
  4. Add any Inset Days (up to 10)
  5. Add leave entries in Toral – Leave and Dale – Leave
  6. View the Year Planner to see the full picture
  7. Check Childcare Overview for coverage gaps
Leave Events

When adding AL entries, select an Event from the dropdown. School holiday events auto-fill start/end dates from your term dates. Select Annual Leave to enter custom dates manually. Use Half Day to deduct 0.5 days.

Childcare Column

In the Year Planner, type anything in the Childcare column to mark that day as childcare arranged. The Childcare Overview counts these automatically.
